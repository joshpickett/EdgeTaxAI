About Us

Learn more about EdgeTaxAI, the team behind it, and our mission to simplify taxes for gig workers and expats.

Our Mission

At EdgeTaxAI, our mission is to make tax management simple, efficient, and accessible for everyone, especially gig workers and expats. We leverage the power of AI to provide cutting-edge solutions that save time, maximize deductions, and minimize tax liabilities.

Our Story

EdgeTaxAI was founded with the belief that modern tax solutions should be as flexible as the workers they serve. Recognizing the unique challenges faced by gig workers and expats, we set out to build a platform that combines advanced technology with personalized support.

Our Team

Our team is made up of tax experts, software engineers, and dedicated support professionals who are passionate about making tax season less stressful and more rewarding for our users.
